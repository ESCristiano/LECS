Information to speed up programming.

	To turn on the leds on the board to do some debug:

		Macros Pins:
			
			__LED_GREEN
			__LED_ORANGE 
			__LED_RED 
			__LED_BLUE 

		Functions:
		
			GPIO_ToggleBits(GPIOD, __LED_GREEN);
			GPIO_WriteBit(GPIOD,__LED_GREEN,Bit_SET);
			GPIO_WriteBit(GPIOD,__LED_GREEN,Bit_RESET);
